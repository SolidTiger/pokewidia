What is Pokéwidia?

Pokéwidia is a website that visualizes data from the Pokémon games in different ways.

The page 'Explore Pokémon' shows the strongest pokemon in different aspects. You can also filter on the different types of
Pokémon and see how they compare to each other.

This website is a project in the course Information Visualization at KTH

This page was created as a project in the course Information Visualization at KTH: Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm.

Create a team!

Visualize the stats of your pokemon team.

The page 'Compare Teams' allows you to create a team of 6 Pokémons and compare their stats.
Create an opposing team and see how your team compares to the other team.
You can also see which Pokémons are stronger than others in the aspect of type advantage.
